Pressure washing across Nutfield

Nutfield is the rural east side of RH1, three miles from our Redhill base. The village character is defined by period properties: cottages clustered around Mid Street and the village centre, larger detached homes along Church Hill with countryside views, and the lower-lying South Nutfield closer to the marsh nature reserve.

Three things define the work here. One is the soil — Surrey clay tracks onto driveways from rural lanes and farm access, and once dry it bonds firmly to paving. Two is the leaf fall — oak, beech and horse chestnut produce tannin staining that won’t come off with a hose. Three is the period material — York stone, sandstone and brick paths and walls that need soft-washing not high pressure.

We cover the rest of RH1 (Redhill, Merstham, Salfords, Earlswood), plus Reigate (RH2), Bletchingley fringe and the rest of Surrey within 20 miles. Full list of areas here.

What we see on Nutfield drives and paths

  • Mud and Surrey clay deposits — from rural lanes, especially in winter. Pressure washing rather than sweeping.
  • Moss on period stone — thick, set in joint pointing. Soft wash with biocide, never high pressure.
  • Leaf tannin staining — oak and beech, dark brown marks on lighter stone.
  • Weathered York stone and sandstone — decades of grime and biological growth. Cleaning reveals the original colour.

Nutfield FAQs

Most-asked questions from Nutfield customers.

How much does driveway cleaning cost in Nutfield?

Most Nutfield drives come in between £80 and £250. Standard RH1 drives are usually £120-£180. Rural drives with longer runs or heavy organic soiling sit higher.

Can you clean natural stone and period surfaces in Nutfield?

Yes — this is our specialism here. Soft wash with low pressure plus pH-neutral cleaners removes moss, algae and lichen without damaging stonework, pointing or historic features.

Do you cover South Nutfield and Kings Cross Lane?

Yes — all of Nutfield including South Nutfield, Mid Street, Church Hill, Nutfield Marsh and Kings Cross Lane. Three miles from Redhill, no travel charge.

How do you deal with mud and clay tracked onto driveways?

Pressure washing — Surrey clay bonds firmly when dried. We blast it off with appropriate nozzle settings, then rinse. Persistent staining gets a mineral pre-treatment first.
