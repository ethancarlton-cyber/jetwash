Common Driveway Problems in Epsom

Epsom homeowners regularly contact us about these issues:

  • Moss and lichen on natural stone: Many Epsom properties feature Indian sandstone, limestone, or slate patios that develop stubborn moss and lichen. The chalk-rich soil and mature tree canopy across the borough accelerate this growth
  • Oil stains on block paving: Block paving driveways across Epsom's residential areas show oil drips, tyre marks, and general staining. We use commercial degreasers to restore the original appearance
  • Green algae on shaded driveways: Tree-lined streets around Ashley Road, Woodcote Green, and the Downs area mean many driveways sit in partial shade, encouraging rapid algae growth that creates slippery, discoloured surfaces. See our guide on how often to schedule driveway cleaning
  • Weathered render on newer homes: Modern developments and new-build estates feature K-rend and painted render that attracts green algae streaks within a few years. We use soft washing techniques to clean these safely
  • Patio slabs lifting and settling: Frost heave and root growth can cause patio slabs to shift over time, trapping moisture and debris in the gaps. Our cleaning helps identify and address these issues early
