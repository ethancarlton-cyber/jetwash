Common Driveway Problems in Crawley

Crawley homeowners regularly contact us about these issues:

  • Block paving moss and weeds: The most common problem across Crawley estates. Block paving driveways in Pound Hill, Furnace Green, and Maidenbower develop thick moss growth between joints, especially on north-facing drives and shaded areas
  • Oil stains on concrete driveways: Older homes around Three Bridges and Northgate typically have concrete driveways that show every oil drip and tyre mark. We use commercial degreasers and hot water treatment to lift stubborn oil stains
  • Green algae on patio slabs: Garden patios collect moisture and organic debris from overhanging trees, creating slippery green films that are both unsightly and a slip hazard
  • Dirty commercial forecourts: Business premises around Manor Royal and County Oak need regular cleaning to maintain a professional image. Chewing gum, oil stains, and general grime build up quickly in high-traffic areas
  • Block paving sinking and shifting: When jointing sand washes out due to weed growth and weathering, blocks become unstable. Our cleaning includes re-sanding to restore a firm, level surface
