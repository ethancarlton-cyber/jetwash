Leatherhead homeowners regularly contact us about these issues:

  • Moss on block paving driveways: Block paving is the dominant surface across Leatherhead's residential estates, and moss growth between joints is the most common complaint. Shaded driveways along Fetcham's tree-lined roads are particularly affected
  • Road dust and pollution staining: Properties near the M25 junction and A24 collect airborne pollution and road dust that gradually darkens driveways and patios. This fine grime embeds in porous surfaces and needs professional treatment

  • Oil stains on older driveways: Concrete and tarmac driveways around the town centre and Kingston Road area show vehicle oil stains that penetrate deep into the surface. We use specialist degreasers and hot water treatment for oil stains
  • Weathered render on new builds: Modern developments around Randalls Park feature rendered facades that attract green streaks within a few years. We use gentle soft washing techniques for these surfaces
