Pressure washing across KT22

Leatherhead is fifteen miles north-west of Redhill, sitting on the River Mole next to the M25 at Junction 9 and the A24 corridor. The KT22 housing mix runs from Georgian and Victorian properties near the town centre through 1930s semis along Kingston Road and Barnett Wood Lane, out to modern executive homes around Randalls Park and the Fetcham borders.

The motorway and A24 mean properties pick up road dust and pollution faster than they would in quieter villages — that fine grey film on driveways and walls. Combined with the River Mole’s influence on humidity and the mature trees lining many residential streets, exterior surfaces accumulate moss and algae quickly. Block paving is the dominant surface across the Fetcham estates and modern Bookham developments.
