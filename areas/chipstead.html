Pressure washing across CR5 Chipstead

Chipstead is six miles north of Redhill, over the North Downs. It’s one of Surrey’s prettier and pricier villages: substantial detached homes along How Lane and Chipstead Valley, period cottages around the village centre near the church, and larger plots heading out toward Shabden Park and Hogcross Lane.

Three things define the work here. One is size — drives of 50 metres or more from road to property aren’t unusual on How Lane. Two is materials — resin-bound, Indian sandstone, limestone and York stone are common, and each takes a different approach. Three is shade — mature oak, beech, silver birch and ornamental trees create dense canopy across most properties, which means heavy moss and lichen on north-facing drives plus enormous leaf fall in autumn.

We cover Banstead (SM7), Kingswood (KT20), Coulsdon, Caterham (CR3), Reigate (RH2) and the rest of Surrey within 20 miles of Redhill. Full list of areas here.

What we see on Chipstead drives and patios

  • Heavy leaf fall on long drives — oak, beech and chestnut produce a leaf-mulch that stains and feeds moss. Pre-treat then power wash.
  • Moss on extensive block paving — How Lane and Chipstead Valley drives can run hundreds of square metres. Commercial-grade kit handles it in one visit.
  • Lichen on natural stone patios — Indian sandstone, limestone, York stone. Soft wash with biocide.
  • Algae on north-facing drives — properties on the north side of east-west streets get minimal direct sun and grow green films fast.

How much does driveway cleaning cost in Chipstead?

Most Chipstead drives come in between £100 and £400. Standard CR5 drives are £140-£220. Larger How Lane and Chipstead Valley drives with premium surfaces sit £220-£400. Free no-obligation quote on any CR5 property.

Do you cover Chipstead Valley and Shabden Park?

Yes — all of CR5 Chipstead including How Lane, Chipstead Valley, Shabden Park, Hogcross Lane, Elmore Road and the village centre. Six miles from Redhill, no travel charge.

Can you clean natural stone driveways and patios in Chipstead?

Yes — Indian sandstone, limestone, travertine and granite are all common. Soft wash on the softer stone, controlled pressure on the harder. Sealing available after cleaning to extend the result.

How do you handle large drives with heavy leaf fall?

Clear loose debris, apply biocidal pre-treatment, allow it to work, then surface-clean systematically. Block paving gets re-sanded. Biocide keeps working for weeks after, slowing regrowth.
