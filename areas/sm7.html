Pressure washing across the whole SM7 postcode

SM7 covers Banstead Village, Nork and Woodmansterne, with bordering KT20 covering Kingswood, Tadworth and Burgh Heath. Eight miles north of our Redhill base, on top of the North Downs.

SM7 housing skews substantial — large detached homes in Kingswood and Tadworth, premium properties along the Banstead-Chipstead corridor, plus the older village fabric around Banstead High Street and Nork. Drives of 60-100m² are normal, and we bring commercial-grade kit so a big resin-bound or block-paving drive doesn’t turn into a two-day job.

The chalk geology drains well, but mature trees through Kingswood Warren and Banstead Woods cast heavy shade and that means moss on every north-facing drive.
