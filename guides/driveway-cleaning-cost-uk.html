Driveway Cleaning Cost UK 2025

Complete price guide: expect to pay £3-6 per square metre, or £100-300 for a typical driveway.

The quick answer: Professional driveway cleaning in the UK costs £3-6 per square metre in 2025. A typical single-car driveway (25sqm) costs £80-150. A double driveway (50sqm) costs £150-300. Prices include high-pressure cleaning and, for block paving, re-sanding of joints.

This guide breaks down exactly what affects pricing, what's included, and how to get the best value for your money.

Prices by Driveway Material

Block Paving

Price: £3.50-6 per sqm

Block paving is the most common driveway type and costs slightly more because joints need re-sanding with kiln-dried sand after cleaning. The sand stabilises blocks, prevents weeds, and discourages ants.

Tarmac/Asphalt

Price: £2.50-4 per sqm

Tarmac is generally the cheapest to clean - no joints mean no re-sanding. However, very old tarmac needs lower pressure to avoid surface damage.

Concrete

Price: £3-5 per sqm

Concrete driveways clean well but may need pre-treatment for oil stains. Older concrete can be porous and may benefit from sealing after cleaning.

Natural Stone

Price: £4-7 per sqm

Natural stone (sandstone, granite, slate) requires more care. Softer stones like sandstone need lower pressure and may take longer. Granite is more robust.

Resin Bound

Price: £3-5 per sqm

Resin driveways are relatively easy to clean and stay cleaner longer. No joints mean no re-sanding required.

What's Included in the Price?

Standard Cleaning Includes:

  • Pre-treatment: Application of moss/algae killer before pressure washing
  • High-pressure cleaning: Professional 3000+ PSI equipment with hot water
  • Rotary surface cleaning: Industrial rotary cleaners for even, streak-free results
  • Edge detailing: Hand cleaning of edges, corners, and around obstacles
  • Re-sanding (block paving): Kiln-dried sand swept into all joints
  • Debris removal: Cleaning up and removing any debris created

Usually Extra:

  • Oil stain treatment: £10-30 per stain depending on size and age
  • Sealing: £3-8 per sqm additional
  • Weed treatment: £15-40 if significant weed growth
  • Moss treatment: £20-50 for heavy moss removal
  • Efflorescence treatment: £30-60 for white salt staining on block paving

Factors That Affect Price

1. Condition of the Driveway

A lightly soiled driveway that was cleaned last year takes less time than one with 5 years of moss buildup. Heavy soiling can add 20-30% to the price.

2. Access

Easy access (equipment can park on or next to the driveway) keeps costs down. Difficult access - carrying equipment through the house, up slopes, or far from water supply - adds time and cost.

3. Location

Prices are higher in London and the South East (£4-7 per sqm) compared to the North (£2.50-4.50 per sqm). Surrey prices typically sit at £3-6 per sqm.

4. Time of Year

Spring is peak season - prices may be slightly higher and availability limited. Winter is quieter but weather can delay jobs.

5. Additional Areas

Combining driveway cleaning with patio, paths, or walls often gets a better per-sqm rate than booking separately.

Is Professional Cleaning Worth It?

Cost Comparison

  • Professional clean (40sqm): £150-240
  • DIY hire + materials: £60-100 + 4-6 hours of your time

Why Professional Is Often Better Value

  • Results last longer: 18-24 months vs 6-12 months for DIY
  • Better equipment: Hot water and higher pressure kill moss at the root
  • No risk of damage: Professionals know correct pressure for each surface
  • Re-sanding included: DIY often skips this critical step
  • Insurance: Any damage is covered
  • Your time: Spend your weekend doing something else

When DIY Makes Sense

  • Very small area (under 15sqm)
  • Light dirt only, no moss
  • You already own a decent pressure washer
  • Tarmac or concrete (no re-sanding needed)
