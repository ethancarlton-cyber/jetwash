Keep Your Gutters Clear and Your Property Protected

Blocked gutters are one of the most common and easily preventable causes of property damage. When gutters overflow, water runs down exterior walls, seeps into brickwork, and can cause damp, mould, and structural damage that costs thousands to repair. We provide professional gutter cleaning in Redhill, Reigate and across Surrey that keeps your drainage system flowing freely, protecting your property year-round.

Surrey's tree-lined streets and leafy suburbs mean gutters fill up faster than in urban areas. Leaves, moss, twigs, and even bird nests can block your gutters and downpipes within a single season. Regular professional cleaning is the most cost-effective way to prevent expensive water damage to your home.

Our Gutter Cleaning Process

1. Inspection & Access Setup

We inspect all accessible gutters from ground level using specialist cameras where needed. We set up safe access using professional ladders or, for higher properties, telescopic gutter vacuum systems that reach up to three storeys.

2. Debris Removal

All leaves, moss, silt, and debris are removed from gutters by hand or using our gutter vacuum system. We bag all waste and dispose of it responsibly - nothing gets left on your property or washed into drains.

3. Downpipe Flushing

Every downpipe is flushed with water to clear any internal blockages. We check flow from top to bottom, ensuring water drains freely through the entire system. Stubborn blockages are cleared using drain rods or high-pressure flushing.

4. Condition Check & Report

We check all gutter joints, brackets, and connections for damage or wear. Any minor repairs are carried out on the spot. We'll let you know about any issues that need further attention, with honest advice on whether repair or replacement is the better option.

Signs Your Gutters Need Cleaning

Many homeowners don't realise their gutters are blocked until damage has already started. Watch for these warning signs across properties in Redhill, Reigate, Dorking, and the wider Surrey area:

  • Water Overflowing During Rain - The most obvious sign. Water cascading over gutter edges means a blockage is preventing normal drainage
  • Sagging Gutters - Debris-filled gutters become heavy and pull away from fascia boards, eventually causing structural damage to the roofline
  • Plants Growing in Gutters - Seeds that land in gutter silt germinate quickly. If you can see plants growing from your gutters, they're severely blocked
  • Staining on Exterior Walls - Green or brown streaks running down walls below the gutter line indicate persistent overflow
  • Damp Patches Inside - Moisture appearing on interior walls near the roofline often traces back to overflowing gutters soaking through brickwork
  • Birds or Wasps Around Gutters - Debris-filled gutters provide ideal nesting material for birds and shelter for wasps

Gutter Maintenance Tips

Between professional cleans, there are steps you can take to keep your gutters functioning well:

  • Annual cleaning - Have gutters professionally cleaned at least once a year, ideally in late autumn after leaf fall
  • Trim overhanging branches - Reducing leaf and debris fall into gutters slows the build-up
  • Check after storms - High winds can deposit debris into gutters. A quick visual check after storms can catch problems early
  • Consider gutter guards - Mesh guards reduce leaf ingress, though they still need periodic cleaning
  • Watch for overflow signs - If you notice water not draining properly during rain, book a clean before damage occurs

Frequently Asked Questions About Gutter Cleaning

How much does gutter cleaning cost in Surrey?

Gutter cleaning in Surrey typically costs £75-150 for a standard residential home. Larger properties, bungalows with difficult access, or properties with severely blocked gutters may cost more. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with our 2-hour callback guarantee. View our full pricing guide.

How often should gutters be cleaned?

We recommend cleaning gutters at least once a year, ideally in late autumn after leaves have fallen. Properties near trees or in wooded areas of Surrey may need cleaning twice a year - once in spring and again in autumn. Regular cleaning prevents blockages, overflows, and costly water damage.

What are the signs of blocked gutters?

Common signs include water overflowing during rain, sagging or pulling away from the fascia, green algae or plant growth in the gutter, water stains on exterior walls below the gutter line, and damp patches on interior walls near the roofline.

Do you also flush downpipes?

Yes, every gutter clean includes thorough downpipe flushing. Blocked downpipes are one of the most common causes of gutter overflow. We flush each downpipe with water to clear debris and confirm free-flowing drainage before completing the job.

Can you repair damaged gutters?

We carry out minor gutter repairs as part of our cleaning service, including resealing leaking joints, reattaching loose brackets, and replacing damaged gutter sections. For major repairs or full gutter replacement, we can advise on the best course of action.
