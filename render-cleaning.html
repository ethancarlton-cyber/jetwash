Expert Render Cleaning & Soft Washing

Rendered walls are prone to green algae growth and black staining, especially in Surrey's damp climate. Our professional render cleaning service uses soft washing techniques to safely remove all organic growth without damaging your render or forcing water into your walls.

Unlike high-pressure washing (which can damage render), soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialist cleaning solutions to kill algae, moss, and bacteria at the root. This provides longer-lasting results and protects your property.

What is Soft Washing?

Soft washing is a gentle cleaning method specifically designed for delicate surfaces like render, painted walls, and building facades. We use:

  • Low Pressure Application - 10-20% of standard pressure washing force
  • Specialist Biocides - Kill algae, moss, and lichen at root level
  • Longer-Lasting Results - Treats the cause, not just the symptoms
  • Safe for Render - No risk of damage or water ingress

Common Render Problems

Green Algae Growth

The most common issue in Surrey. Green algae (Gloeocapsa magma) thrives on north-facing and shaded walls, creating unsightly green or black streaks.

Black Staining

Dark streaks caused by algae, pollution, and organic matter. Particularly noticeable on white or light-coloured renders.

Red Algae

Less common but creates distinctive red/pink staining on render surfaces.

Frequently Asked Questions About Render Cleaning

What is the difference between soft washing and pressure washing for render?

Soft washing uses low pressure (10-20% of standard pressure washing) combined with specialist biocide cleaning solutions. This kills algae and organic growth at root level without risking damage to delicate render surfaces. High-pressure washing can damage render, force water into walls, and only removes surface growth without treating the cause.

Is soft washing safe for K-Rend and other modern renders?

Yes, soft washing is the recommended cleaning method for K-Rend and all modern acrylic renders. The low-pressure application and specialist cleaning solutions safely remove algae and staining without damaging the render texture or coating. We also clean traditional sand/cement render, pebbledash, and painted render safely.

How long does render cleaning take?

Most residential properties take half a day to a full day for render cleaning, depending on the size of the property and extent of algae growth. The cleaning solution needs 15-30 minutes dwell time to penetrate and kill organic growth before rinsing, which ensures longer-lasting results.

How much does render cleaning cost in Surrey?

Render cleaning prices depend on the property size and extent of algae growth. Typical costs range from £200-500 for a semi-detached house and £300-700 for a detached property. View our pricing guide or request a free quote for your specific property.

How long do render cleaning results last?

Soft washing results typically last 2-4 years, significantly longer than pressure washing alone. Because our biocide treatment kills algae at root level rather than just removing surface growth, regrowth is much slower. North-facing walls and heavily shaded areas may need more frequent cleaning.
