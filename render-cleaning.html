How a render soft wash works

Four steps. The biocide does the work, not the pressure.

Protect

Plants and lawns wetted thoroughly. Delicate borders sheeted. Windows and doors checked for sealing.

Apply biocide

Specialist soft-wash solution applied at low pressure. Penetrates the algae and lichen and kills at root level.

Dwell

15–30 minutes for the biocide to do its job. This is the bit that gives you 2–4 years instead of 6 months.

Rinse & finish

Low-pressure rinse from top down. Garden checked, surrounding areas rinsed. Render comes back to original colour.

Why soft wash, not pressure wash, on render

Render — especially K-Rend and other modern silicone renders — is not a surface that takes pressure. Hit it with a normal pressure washer and you’ll strip the texture, force water in behind the render, and end up with a worse problem six months later when the algae is back. Soft wash is the only correct method. Low pressure, biocide, dwell, rinse. The biocide does the killing, not the water.

Render cleaning FAQs

Most-asked questions from Surrey customers.

What’s the difference between soft washing and pressure washing for render?

Soft wash is low pressure plus biocide that kills algae at root level. Pressure wash on render strips the surface, forces water into the wall behind, and only knocks the green off so it grows back in months. Soft wash is the only correct method.

Is soft washing safe for K-Rend?

Yes — soft wash is the manufacturer-recommended method for K-Rend, silicone render, acrylic and pebbledash. We clean to spec.

How long does render cleaning take?

Half a day to a full day for most homes. The biocide needs 15–30 minutes dwell time before we rinse — that’s what gives results that last 2–4 years.

How much does render cleaning cost in Surrey?

£200 small terraced front. £250–£500 semi-detached. £300–£700 detached, depending on size and how high the gables are.

How long do render cleaning results last?

2–4 years on most properties. North-facing and shaded walls may need re-doing sooner.
