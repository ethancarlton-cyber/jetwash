Calculator FAQs

The questions we get asked about pressure washing prices.

How much does pressure washing cost per square metre?

Basic pressure washing typically costs £3–£8 per m² in the UK. With sealing or specialist treatments, expect £5–£15 per m². Full breakdown in our UK jet washing prices guide.

How much for an average driveway?

An average UK driveway (~40m²) is £160–£400. Most fall in the £200–£350 range depending on surface and condition. Compare materials in our UK driveway types comparison.

Is there a minimum charge?

Yes — £80 minimum for most professionals (us included). It covers travel, equipment setup and the base work. See basic vs premium tiers.

Does surface condition affect the price?

Yes. Light soiling = standard rate, moderate adds about 20%, heavy/neglected can add up to 50% due to extra time and pre-treatment. Realistic results in our before & after expectations guide.

How accurate is this calculator?

It’s a useful estimate based on UK 2026 market rates. Actual cost depends on access, exact condition and local pricing. For an exact price, request a free quote — or check our Surrey market comparison.
