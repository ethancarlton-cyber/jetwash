UK Pressure Washing Price Guide 2026

Typical costs for professional pressure washing services across the UK, based on current market rates.

Driveway Cleaning

Average UK driveway (~40m²): £100 - £400. Cost depends on surface type (block paving, tarmac, concrete), condition, and size. Block paving typically costs more due to joint re-sanding.

Patio Cleaning

Average UK patio (~20m²): £80 - £250. Natural stone, concrete slabs, and porcelain all respond well to professional cleaning. Black spot treatment may add to the cost.

Block Paving

Standard block paved area: £100 - £400+. Includes weed removal and cleaning. Re-sanding joints and sealing are recommended extras that protect your investment long-term.

Decking

Average decking (~15m²): £100 - £300. Both wood and composite decking benefit from professional cleaning. Oiling or staining after cleaning is a popular add-on service.

Render / Walls

Typical house render: £150 - £500+. Soft washing technique is used to safely clean rendered walls without damage. Includes biocide treatment to prevent regrowth.

Commercial

Commercial properties: From £200+. Car parks, forecourts, warehouse yards, and shop fronts. Regular maintenance contracts offer better value for ongoing cleaning needs.

Pressure Washing Cost FAQs

How much does pressure washing cost per square metre?

Basic pressure washing typically costs £3-£8 per m² in the UK. With additional treatments like sealing, costs range from £5-£15 per m². The exact rate depends on the surface type and condition.

How much does it cost to pressure wash an average driveway?

An average UK driveway (around 40m²) costs between £160 and £400 to pressure wash. Most standard driveway cleans fall in the £200-£350 range, depending on surface type and condition.

Is there a minimum charge for pressure washing?

Yes, most professionals have a minimum charge of £80-£100. This covers equipment setup, travel, and the base work involved regardless of area size.

Does surface condition affect the price?

Yes. Lightly soiled surfaces cost the standard rate. Moderate soiling adds around 20% to the price. Heavily neglected surfaces can cost up to 50% more due to extra time and pre-treatment needed.
