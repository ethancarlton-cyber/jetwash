How a commercial clean works

Four steps. Built around your trading hours.

Site visit

Free site walk-around. We measure, identify problem areas (oil, gum, food residue), check access and water supply, agree the schedule.

Written quote

Detailed written quote — one-off cleans plus contract options. Insurance certificate available on request before work starts.

Out-of-hours clean

Evenings, weekends or early mornings — whatever fits your operation. Commercial-grade equipment, proper flow rates for large areas.

Sign-off & schedule

Walk-through with the duty manager when we’re done. Contracts get scheduled visits booked in for the year ahead.

Why presentation pays for itself

A grimy forecourt or stained car park is the first thing your customer sees. It tells them what to expect inside. A weekly contract clean is cheaper per visit than a one-off, keeps the site looking right for every customer, and reads as proper professional standards on health-and-safety inspections. Most retail and hospitality clients pay back the contract cost in keeping one regular customer who would otherwise have walked.

Commercial cleaning FAQs

Most-asked questions from Surrey businesses.

Do you offer out-of-hours cleaning?

Yes — evenings, weekends, overnight. Most retail and hospitality clients prefer overnight cleans so the site is fresh for first customer without losing trading hours.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes — monthly, quarterly, annual. Contract pricing typically 15–25% lower than one-off rates. Best fit for forecourts, restaurants, car parks, multi-let sites.

Can you handle large car parks and yards?

Yes — commercial-grade ride-on surface cleaners. Oil staining, tyre marks, gum, food residue. Out-of-hours scheduling so no customer disruption.

How much does commercial pressure washing cost?
