Frequently Asked Questions About Commercial Pressure Washing

Do you offer out-of-hours commercial cleaning?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including evenings, weekends, and early mornings to minimise disruption to your business operations. Many of our commercial clients prefer overnight or weekend cleaning so their premises are spotless when staff and customers arrive without any operational downtime.

Do you offer maintenance contracts for regular commercial cleaning?

Yes, we offer monthly, quarterly, and annual maintenance contracts with preferential rates. Regular cleaning contracts are ideal for businesses that need consistent presentation, such as retail premises, car parks, and hospitality venues. Contract pricing is typically 15-25% lower than one-off cleaning rates.

Can you clean large car parks and parking areas?

Yes, we have commercial-grade equipment capable of handling large car parks, loading bays, and industrial yards. We remove oil stains, tyre marks, chewing gum, and general dirt from all parking surfaces. Out-of-hours cleaning ensures no disruption to your customers or operations.

How much does commercial pressure washing cost?

Commercial pricing is based on area size, cleaning frequency, and specific requirements. One-off cleaning typically costs £2-5 per square metre, while contract pricing offers significant savings. View our pricing guide or request a free commercial quote.

Are you fully insured for commercial work?

Yes, we carry comprehensive public liability insurance that covers all commercial cleaning work. We can provide certificates of insurance upon request for your records or to satisfy your insurance requirements. Our insurance protects both your property and our team during all work.
