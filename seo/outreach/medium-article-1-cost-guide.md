# How Much Does Pressure Washing Cost in Surrey? (2026 Complete Guide)

*Last updated: February 2026*

If you're a homeowner in Surrey wondering how much it costs to have your driveway, patio, or exterior surfaces professionally cleaned, you're not alone. Pressure washing prices vary depending on surface type, condition, and the size of the area, so getting a clear picture before you commit is important.

This guide breaks down the typical costs for every major surface type in the Surrey area, what affects the price, and how to get the best value for your money.

## Driveway Cleaning Costs

Driveways are the most commonly cleaned surface in Surrey, and for good reason. Block paving, tarmac, and concrete driveways all accumulate dirt, moss, oil stains, and algae over time, especially in our damp climate.

**Typical driveway cleaning costs in Surrey:**

| Surface Type | Price Per m² | Typical Driveway (40-60m²) |
|---|---|---|
| Tarmac | £4 - £6 | £160 - £360 |
| Concrete | £4 - £7 | £160 - £420 |
| Block Paving | £5 - £9 | £200 - £540 |
| Block Paving (with re-sanding) | £7 - £12 | £280 - £720 |

Block paving is extremely common across Surrey developments, particularly in Redhill, Reigate, and newer estates in Horley and Crawley. It tends to cost slightly more because weeds need to be removed from joints and re-sanding is often recommended to keep the paving stable.

**Oil stain treatment** is usually included in the base price when you use a professional service, though severely stained areas may incur a small surcharge for specialist degreaser.

## Patio Cleaning Costs

Patios are the second most popular surface for pressure washing. Natural stone patios, particularly Indian sandstone, are widespread across Surrey properties and are prone to black spot (lichen) buildup.

**Typical patio cleaning costs:**

| Surface Type | Price Per m² |
|---|---|
| Concrete slabs | £3 - £5 |
| Natural stone (sandstone, limestone) | £4 - £7 |
| Porcelain tiles | £3 - £6 |
| Indian stone with black spot treatment | £5 - £8 |

Black spot removal on natural stone requires specialist biocide treatment before pressure washing. This is a common issue on patios across Surrey, especially in shaded gardens or north-facing areas where moisture lingers.

Optional sealant application after cleaning typically adds £3-£5 per square metre but helps protect the surface and keeps it cleaner for longer.

## Render Cleaning Costs

Rendered walls are increasingly common on Surrey homes, particularly newer builds in areas like Banstead, Epsom, and Crawley. Green algae and black staining on render not only looks unsightly but can damage the surface over time.

**Render cleaning typically costs £8 - £15 per square metre.** This is higher than ground-level surfaces because it requires soft washing (low-pressure application with specialist biocide) rather than high-pressure jets, which could damage the render.

A typical two-storey house front might cost between £300 and £600, depending on the extent of staining and the height of the walls.

## Decking Cleaning Costs

Wooden and composite decking gets slippery and discoloured with algae growth, particularly through Surrey's wet autumn and winter months.

**Decking cleaning costs £4 - £8 per square metre.** A typical garden deck of 15-25m² would cost between £60 and £200. Oiling or staining after cleaning is available as an optional extra, usually adding £4-£6 per square metre.

## Commercial Pressure Washing

For businesses, car parks, forecourts, and warehouse yards, commercial pressure washing starts from around £200 and is usually quoted on a case-by-case basis. Regular maintenance contracts often come with discounted rates.

## What Affects the Price?

Several factors influence the final cost of pressure washing:

**1. Size of the area** - This is the biggest factor. Larger areas cost more in total but usually have a lower per-square-metre rate.

**2. Surface condition** - A lightly soiled driveway that was cleaned last year will cost less than one that hasn't been touched in five years and is covered in deep-rooted moss and oil stains.

**3. Surface type** - Some materials require more careful handling. Natural stone and render need gentler techniques, while concrete can withstand higher pressure.

**4. Access** - Easy, ground-level access with space for equipment keeps costs down. Difficult access, steps, or narrow passages may add to the price.

**5. Additional services** - Re-sanding block paving joints, applying sealant, or treating specific stains are typically quoted as extras on top of the base cleaning price.

**6. Location** - Most Surrey-based services include travel within their core area at no extra charge. Properties on the outskirts of a company's service radius may see a small travel surcharge.

## Is Professional Pressure Washing Worth It?

In short, yes. Professional equipment delivers 3000+ PSI of pressure with heated water and rotary surface cleaners, which simply can't be matched by a rented domestic machine. The results are more even, longer-lasting, and completed in a fraction of the time.

Professional operators also know how to adjust pressure for different surfaces, preventing the kind of damage that can happen with DIY pressure washing (etched concrete, displaced jointing sand, or cracked slabs from too much pressure).

For most Surrey homeowners, having their driveway and patio professionally cleaned every 12-18 months is the most cost-effective approach to maintaining their property's appearance and value.
