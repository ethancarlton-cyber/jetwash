What affects the price

Same quote, different drive, here’s what moves the number up or down.

Size

We measure in square metres. A standard single drive is 30–50 m². Larger areas drop the per-m² rate.

Surface

Block paving, sandstone, concrete, render, decking, each takes a different technique and a different rate.

Condition

Heavy moss, oil staining, lichen or stubborn black-spot need extra pre-treatment time. Light cleans are quicker.

Access

Easy water access and parking keeps the price down. Awkward back gardens or no tap on-site adds a small amount.

Extras

Sealing, oiling, soft-wash biocide, post-clean re-sanding on patios, quoted separately so you only pay for what you ask for.

Location

No travel charge inside our core area (Redhill, Reigate, Horley, Dorking, Banstead). Outer-radius jobs, same rate.

Pricing FAQs

The questions we get asked most often before quoting.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. We discount when you bundle services on the same visit (driveway plus patio is the common one), for regular maintenance contracts, and on larger jobs where the m² rate drops. Just ask when we quote. For wider context, see our UK jet washing prices breakdown.

Can I get a quote without a site visit?

For most jobs, yes. Approximate measurements and a few photos are usually enough to quote accurately by phone or online. For complex or unusual jobs, a brief site visit makes the quote more reliable. The cost calculator gives a rough estimate in 30 seconds, or compare our basic, premium and full-restoration tiers.

How do Surrey prices compare to the rest of the UK?

Surrey rates sit slightly above the UK average because labour costs are higher in the South East, but they’re in line with neighbouring counties. We benchmark against the Surrey market and national jet washing ranges to keep our prices fair.

Do you charge for quotes?

No. Every quote is free with no obligation to proceed. There’s no call-out fee either, even if we have to come out for a site visit.

When do I pay?

Payment is due on completion of work. Cash, bank transfer and card all accepted. No deposits required for residential jobs.

What if I’m not happy with the result?

If the result isn’t right, we redo it free. We’d rather come back and fix it than leave you with a finish that doesn’t hold up to the quote.
