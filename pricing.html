What Affects the Price?

Size

Larger areas naturally cost more. We measure in square meters and price accordingly.

Condition

Heavily soiled surfaces or stubborn stains require more time and specialist treatment, affecting cost.

Surface Type

Different materials (block paving, natural stone, render) require different techniques and may have varying costs.

Access

Easy access keeps costs down. Difficult-to-reach areas may cost slightly more.

Additional Services

Re-sanding, sealing, specialist stain treatment are quoted separately and added to base cost.

Location

Properties in our core service area (Redhill, Reigate, Horley, Dorking) have no travel surcharge.

For many jobs, yes. If you can provide approximate measurements and photos, we can usually quote accurately over the phone or online. For complex jobs or unusual circumstances, a brief site visit ensures accurate pricing.
