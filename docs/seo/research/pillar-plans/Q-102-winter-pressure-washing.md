# Q-102 — Winter pressure washing UK frost-day rules

## Stance (60-word answer-first)

Consumer-grade Karcher K7 and similar machines are rated for use down to ambient 5°C; below that, water in the pump and hose risks freezing during pauses. More importantly, soaked water in the jointing sand freezes and expands — Marshalls cleaning guidance warns against treating paving with frost in the substrate or forecast in the next 24 hours. BS 7533-101 treats the jointing sand as load-bearing. Met Office Wisley records Surrey at 32–38 air-frost days/year.
