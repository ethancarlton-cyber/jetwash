# Same Day Jet Wash — 12-Month SEO Roadmap (Jul 2026 → Jun 2027)

**Goal:** maximise enquiries — fully optimise existing services for SEO *and* win new-service demand across Surrey/Sussex.
**Authored:** 2026-06-21 · feeds `tracker.json` and the `/seo` + `/research-loop` + `/fe` skills.

---

## 0. Where we already are (so we plan from reality, not zero)

The site is **not a cold start**. It's a mature, technically excellent estate:

- **124 live pages**: 12 service pages, 62 research-grade guides (≥1,500 words, 5+ citations), 22 area pages, 12 service×city pages, calculator, quote funnel.
- **Tech:** static HTML on Cloudflare Workers, 25ms TTFB, clean URLs, full schema (LocalBusiness/Service/FAQ/Breadcrumb), sitemap, robots (AI crawlers allowed), NAP consistent (01737 652 515, Redhill RH1).
- **Voice system:** voice.md/humor.md/opinions.md built + ZeroGPT-validated (0–7.6% AI). **stats.md + stories.md incomplete — need Ethan's real numbers/stories.**
- **North Star (in tracker):** be the most-cited UK pressure-washing authority in AI answers — target 40/50 priority queries cited by 2026-12-31. Currently **0 verified citations**.
- **The real bottleneck is NOT content volume.** Two things are throttling enquiries:
  1. **Conversion** — 76% bounce on `/quote`, only ~2/50 guide sessions reach it, **0 real customer conversions** logged since 2026-06-18.
  2. **Local pack / reviews** — review velocity + GBP are the #1 lever for local-service enquiries *and* for AI citations; this is under-built relative to content.

**Strategic consequence:** the roadmap is NOT "write 200 more pages." It rebalances effort into **reviews + conversion + high-value targeted expansion**, while running the content engine on a seasonal slow-ramp. More traffic into a leaky funnel is wasted spend.

## 1. The model (from the SEO playbook)

Two coupled motions:
1. **Service pages** (commercial intent, service × city) — where enquiries convert. Treat as CRO landing pages.
2. **Blog/guides** (informational) — raise topical authority + win AI citations + feed top-of-funnel.

Plus three force-multipliers the playbook + research make non-negotiable for a *local* business:
- **A. Reviews/GBP engine** — biggest local-pack + AI-citation lever (GBP signals ~32% of local ranking; AI tools cite GBP first; rating floors: ChatGPT 4.3★, 40+ reviews to crack pack in mid towns).
- **B. Conversion (CRO)** — fix the funnel before pouring in traffic.
- **C. Publish-ahead seasonality** — SEO takes 6–12 weeks to rank, so publish 2–3 months *before* each demand wave.

**Off-page SEO: skip** (PBNs/paid links = ban risk). Earn citations + "best-of" mentions instead.

## 3. Priority targets (the "what", before the "when")

### 3a. Anchor service order (per town)
**gutter → driveway → patio → roof/render.** Gutter is the softest niche in almost every town (easiest to rank, anchors bundling). Driveway = #1 demand. Roof/render = highest ticket — lead with these in affluent towns.

### 3b. Priority NEW towns (within ~20 mi, affluent, weak rivals) — build these first
**Money zone:** Kingswood · Walton-on-the-Hill · Tadworth · Coulsdon · Ewell · Cheam (+ deepen existing Caterham, Banstead, Horley).
**Volume plays:** Sutton · Purley · Ashtead · Great Bookham · Lingfield.
**Marginal (long-tail only):** East Grinstead, Horsham, Haywards Heath (gutter angle), Cobham. **Skip Burgess Hill** (too far, least affluent).

### 3c. Top 25 service×city pages to build first
Affluent + weak competition + high ticket. Sequence: driveway → gutter → roof/render.
Kingswood (driveway/roof) · Coulsdon (driveway/gutter/render/roof) · Ewell (driveway/patio/gutter) · Cheam (patio/gutter) · Tadworth (driveway) · Walton-on-the-Hill (driveway) · Banstead (driveway/patio) · Caterham (driveway/gutter) · Horley (driveway/gutter) · Reigate (gutter/render/roof) · Purley (driveway) · Sutton (driveway) · Oxted (driveway) · Lingfield (driveway) · Ashtead (driveway). **Gutter everywhere as the low-resistance wedge.**

### 3d. New SERVICE pages worth adding
- **Artificial-grass / astroturf cleaning** — best new homeowner page (same kit, affluent Surrey, recurring pet-owner demand). £99–250/job.
- **Conservatory roof cleaning** — low-competition niche, "can't safely DIY" hook, bundles with gutter/window. £100–500.
- **B2B silo (SEO + outreach):** car-dealership forecourt cleaning, fleet/vehicle washing, playground/MUGA/3G cleaning. Low volume, four-figure recurring contracts. Needs CHAS/SafeContractor + outreach, not just SEO.
- **Brick cleaning** (façade refresh + new-build cement-smear B2B).
- **Skip oven/BBQ cleaning** (indoor, zero kit/SEO overlap).

### 3e. Blog gaps competitors don't own (RTB-first)
Lead with ready-to-buy problem-diagnosis content (self-disqualifies DIY → books a pro):
render-damage rescue · can-you-pressure-wash-K-Rend · roof black-streaks (UK tiles, not US shingles) · lichen-vs-moss · gutters→internal-damp · re-sand-after-cleaning · resin-driveway-gone-green · efflorescence · solar-ROI-math · sell-house-kerb-appeal · landlord-vs-tenant-law · how-to-avoid-cowboys · composite-decking (gap) · painted-masonry soft-wash (gap). **Bolt on the Surrey local hook** (clay soil, north-facing/tree-shaded plots, deciduous leaf litter) — no competitor owns the localised cut.

## 4. Seasonality engine (the publishing clock)

**Publish 2–3 months AHEAD of each wave.** Surrey fires ~2 weeks early.

| Win demand in… | Publish in… | Push these services |
|---|---|---|
| **Autumn (Sep–Nov)** — gutter peak | **Jun–Aug** | Gutters, fascia/soffit, roof/moss pre-frost |
| **Winter/Festive (Dec)** | **Sep–Oct** | Pre-Christmas tidy, last gutter clear, off-peak offers |
| **Spring (Mar–May)** — BIGGEST wave | **Dec–Feb** | Driveway, patio, decking, render, roof, soft-wash, solar, moss, pre-sale kerb appeal |
| **Summer (Jun–Aug)** | **Apr–Jun** | Windows, solar, patios, commercial forecourts |

All service×city builds gated on Ethan's photo approval (illustrative vs real).

## 7. Guardrails (don't undo the good work)

- **No volume spikes** — steady 3–5/week, never dump batches.
- **Depth > breadth** — keep the ≥1,500-word research standard; reject thin pages.
- **Voice first** — every new page runs the ZeroGPT check; no AI-slop.
- **Honest imagery** — illustrative before/after labelled as such; never fake "our work."
- **No fabricated metrics/reviews** — only real, reproducible numbers (see global rule).
- **No off-page link-buying** — earn citations instead.
- **Service-area realism** — don't claim towns outside the ~20-mi radius.
- **Cap service×city restraint** — hundreds is the ceiling, not thousands; quality per town.
