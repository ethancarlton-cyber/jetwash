2 The Problem

4 months live. 68 pages. 9 SEO phases completed. Zero enquiries via the web form.

Diagnosis

  • Ranking well for informational keywords — "how much does driveway cleaning cost", "pressure washing vs jet washing", "when to clean your patio". These attract researchers, not buyers.
  • Area page titles were informational — "Jetwashing Redhill (RH1) | Pressure Washing" does not signal that you can book a service here.
  • No pricing tables — all 22 area pages used bullet-point pricing lists, which AI systems and users both find harder to parse and compare.
  • No booking process — visitors landing on area pages had no clear visual path from "I'm interested" to "I've submitted a request".
  • Business model misunderstanding — the site was being treated as an anonymous lead-gen model. In reality, the GBP listing is active and the phone number (01737 652 515) is intentionally prominent. Previous sessions removed the phone number twice before this was clarified.
  • No AI visibility — only 11 AI crawlers were allowed, dateModified on guides was 47 days stale, and zero third-party content (Medium, LinkedIn) existed for AI cross-referencing.

3 Strategic Pivot: Four Pillars

The pivot rests on four pillars, each supported by specific research findings from the session's four research agents.

1 AI Citation Optimization (GEO/AEO)

45% of consumers now use AI to find local services. AI referral traffic converts at 14.2% vs 2.8% for organic (5x better). Getting cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini bypasses the GBP/map pack problem.

  • ChatGPT uses Bing, not Google for live queries
  • 85% of AI citations come from third-party pages
  • Schema markup = 3x more likely to earn AI citations
  • Tables cited 2.5x more than prose; listicles get 74.2%
  • Statistics every 150–200 words = 40% improvement
  • Content not updated quarterly = 3x more likely to lose citations

2 Transactional Intent Conversion

Stop attracting researchers, start capturing buyers. Transactional pages convert at 5–15% vs 0.5–2% for informational — a 5–10x improvement.

  • Interactive calculators convert 16.9x better than static forms (47.3% vs 2.8%)
  • Google's "Online estimates" filter shows ABOVE the map pack
  • Long-tail transactional keywords often don't trigger local pack
  • Location-specific pages convert 2–3x better
  • "Book" / "hire" / "quote" modifiers = highest purchase intent

3 Competitive Exploitation

Local tradesmen have GBP but terrible websites. We exploit every gap they leave open.

  • #1 competitor has ~10 pages vs our 68
  • No competitor has an interactive calculator
  • No competitor has comprehensive guides
  • No competitor has AI-optimized content
  • 32 companies on Checkatrade, 761 reviews on Bark — aggregators dominate
  • Brand mentions are 3x more influential than backlinks for ChatGPT

4 Linkable Research Assets

Create deeply researched, data-rich pillar content that becomes THE UK reference. Original research earns 4.7x more backlinks and 40% more AI visibility.

  • Only 1 UK pressure washing statistics page exists online
  • 95% of pages have zero backlinks — one strong link wins
  • Authoritative list mentions = 41% of influence on ChatGPT
  • Deep topical coverage rewarded by March 2026 Core Update
  • Key data available: £27.5B market, 65/35 resi/comm split, 70% seasonal

5 Research Findings

Four research agents ran at the start of the session to inform the strategic pivot. Key findings below.

Agent 1: AI Search Optimization

  • ChatGPT uses Bing, not Google for live queries — Bing visibility is critical and was completely missing (no Bing Webmaster Tools registered)
  • 85% of AI citations come from third-party pages (Medium, directories, roundups) — on-site optimization alone is insufficient
  • Pages with schema markup are 3x more likely to earn AI citations
  • Only 38% of citations come from top-10 Google results — AI draws from much broader sources
  • AI referral traffic converts at 14.2% vs 2.8% organic — 5x better conversion rate

Agent 2: Transactional Intent

  • Interactive calculators convert at 47.3% vs 2.8% for static forms — a 16.9x improvement
  • Google's "Online estimates" filter appears above the map pack — our calculator qualifies, no competitor's does
  • Transactional pages convert 5–15% vs 0.5–2% for informational
  • Location-specific pages convert 2–3x better than generic pages
  • "Book" / "hire" / "get a quote" modifiers signal the highest purchase intent in search

Agent 3: Competitive Hacks

  • Brand mentions are 3x more influential than backlinks for ChatGPT recommendations
  • Reddit is the #2 most-cited site by AI systems — opportunity for organic community engagement
  • Wikidata entries improve Knowledge Graph presence, which feeds AI entity understanding
  • Authoritative list mentions account for 41% of influence on ChatGPT recommendations
  • Competitor content is thin (<10 pages vs our 68), no calculators, no AI optimization

Agent 4: Q1–Q2 2026 Trends

  • Only 38% of AI citations come from pages in Google's top 10 — being #1 on Google is not enough for AI visibility
  • March 2026 Core Update explicitly rewards information gain — original research and unique data win
  • AI referral traffic converts 2x better than traditional organic traffic
  • FAQ/HowTo rich results reduced on non-primary content pages, but schema still helps AI comprehension
  • April 2026 Core Update is rolling out right now — our changes align with what it rewards
