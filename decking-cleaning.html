Decking Types We Clean

Softwood Decking

Most common type in UK gardens. Requires careful pressure washing to avoid damaging the softer wood. We restore the golden/honey colour and can prepare for oiling.

Hardwood Decking

Our Decking Cleaning Process

1. Assessment

Identify decking type and condition, check for loose boards or damage.

2. Dry Brush

Remove loose debris, leaves, and surface dirt before washing.

3. Pre-Treatment

Apply specialist deck cleaner to break down organic growth and embedded dirt.

4. Pressure Washing

Frequently Asked Questions About Decking Cleaning

Can you clean composite decking as well as wooden decking?

Yes, we clean both composite and wooden decking. Composite decking requires gentler pressure settings to avoid surface damage, while wooden decking needs appropriate pressure for the wood type (softwood vs hardwood). We adjust our equipment and technique for each decking material to ensure safe, effective cleaning.

Can I stain or oil my decking after you clean it?

Yes, professional cleaning is the ideal preparation for staining or oiling wooden decking. The deck must be completely dry before applying treatment - typically 2-3 days of dry weather after cleaning. We offer deck oiling and staining as an optional add-on service, or you can apply treatment yourself once the deck has dried.

How long does it take for decking to dry after cleaning?

Decking typically takes 24-48 hours to dry fully after pressure washing, depending on weather conditions. You can walk on the decking immediately after cleaning, but wait until it's completely dry before applying any oil, stain, or sealant. Allow 2-3 days of dry weather before treatment application.

How much does decking cleaning cost?

Decking cleaning in Redhill and Reigate typically costs £5-10 per square metre, depending on the decking type and condition. A standard 15-20 sqm deck costs around £75-150 for cleaning. Oil or stain application is quoted separately. View our pricing guide or request a free quote.

Will pressure washing damage my wooden decking?

Not when done by professionals using correct techniques. We use appropriate pressure settings for your decking type, always work with the grain direction, and apply specialist deck cleaners to loosen dirt before washing. This ensures thorough cleaning without splintering or damaging the wood surface. Learn more about safe cleaning methods in our guide to pressure washing damage prevention.
