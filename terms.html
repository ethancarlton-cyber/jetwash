Our services

Same Day JetWash provides professional pressure washing and exterior cleaning services, including but not limited to:

  • Driveway cleaning and sealing

Submitting a quote request through our website does not constitute a confirmed booking. A booking is only confirmed once we have agreed a date, time, and price with you directly. We aim to respond to all quote requests within 2 hours during working hours (Mon–Sat 8am–6pm).

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule a booking due to adverse weather conditions, equipment failure, or other circumstances beyond our control. In such cases, we will contact you to arrange an alternative date at no extra cost.

Payment is due upon completion of the work unless otherwise agreed in advance. We accept payment by bank transfer, cash or card. For larger jobs, we may require a deposit before work commences.
