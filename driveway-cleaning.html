How much does driveway cleaning cost in Redhill & Reigate?

Driveway cleaning prices in Redhill and Reigate typically range from £3-6 per square metre, depending on the surface type, condition, and size. A standard 40-50 sqm driveway costs around £120-250. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with our 2-hour callback guarantee. View our full pricing guide.

How long does it take to clean a driveway?

Most residential driveways take 2-4 hours to clean professionally. Larger driveways or those with heavy staining, oil marks, or extensive moss growth may take longer. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when we provide your quote.

Can you remove oil stains from my driveway?

Yes, we can remove most oil stains from driveways. We use specialist pre-treatment solutions designed to break down oil and grease before pressure washing. Fresh stains remove completely, while older, deeply absorbed stains may be significantly reduced but not always 100% removed.

How often should I have my driveway cleaned?

We recommend professional driveway cleaning every 1-2 years in Surrey, depending on conditions. Driveways in shaded areas or under trees may need annual cleaning due to faster moss and algae growth. Regular cleaning prevents long-term damage and keeps your property looking its best. Read our detailed guide on how often you should clean your driveway for more advice.

Do you clean all types of driveways in Surrey?

Yes, we clean all driveway types including block paving, tarmac, concrete, pattern imprinted concrete, and resin-bound surfaces. Each material requires different pressure settings and techniques, which our experienced team adjusts to ensure safe, effective cleaning without surface damage.
