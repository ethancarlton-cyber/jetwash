How much does driveway cleaning cost in Surrey?

Driveway cleaning costs in Surrey vary by size, surface type and condition, most jobs come in at £120–£250 for a clean, hypo treatment to kill the moss spores, plus 4–6 bags of fresh kiln dried sand brushed back into the joints. Smaller paths from £80. Bigger driveways with oil stain treatment, render, or full re-sanding go up to £400–£500. Anyone offering ‘£60 for any driveway’ has skipped the re-sand. We provide free, no-obligation quotes, phone 01737 652 515 and we’ll call back within 2 hours during business hours (Mon–Sat 8am–6pm).

How often should I have my driveway cleaned?

In Surrey’s damp climate, professional driveway cleaning every 18–24 months keeps moss, algae and weed buildup in check for most homes. Shaded driveways or those under mature trees may need cleaning annually, moss thrives on cool damp brick and the bits the sun never reaches need attention sooner. Open south-facing tarmac can stretch three years. Anyone telling you ‘every spring’ regardless is selling annual contracts you don’t actually need.
