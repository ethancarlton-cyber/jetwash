## TECHNICAL SEO IMPLEMENTATION

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**Every Page Must Have:**
- [ ] Unique, keyword-rich `<title>` tag (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Compelling meta description (150-160 characters)
- [ ] Single H1 with primary keyword
- [ ] H2/H3 subheadings with semantic keywords
- [ ] Alt text on all images (descriptive + keyword)
- [ ] Internal links to related pages
- [ ] Clear CTA above the fold
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Fast load time (<3 seconds)
- [ ] HTTPS (automatic with free hosting)

**Title Tag Formula:**
```
[Service] in [Location] | [Business Name] | [USP]

Example:
"Driveway Cleaning in Haywards Heath | JetWash Pros | Free Quotes"
```

**Meta Description Formula:**
```
Professional [service] in [location]. [Benefit]. [Benefit]. Free quotes. Call [phone] or book online today.

Example:
"Professional driveway cleaning in Haywards Heath & West Sussex. Transform your property. Same-day quotes. Fully insured. Call 07XXX XXXXXX for your free quote."
```

## CONTENT WRITING GUIDELINES

### Tone & Style
- **Professional but approachable** - Not corporate, friendly local business
- **Benefit-focused** - What customer gets, not just what you do
- **Trust-building** - Address concerns (damage, mess, cost)
- **Local references** - Mention specific areas, types of homes
- **Action-oriented** - Clear CTAs throughout

### Keyword Density
- **Primary keyword:** 1-2% density
- **Natural placement** - Never forced or awkward
- **Semantic variations** - Use synonyms (jetwashing, jet washing, pressure washing)
- **LSI keywords** - Related terms (cleaning, washing, restoration)

### Content Formula (Service Pages)

**Structure:**
1. **Introduction (100-150 words)**
   - What the service is
   - Why it's needed in this area
   - Primary keyword in first sentence

2. **Benefits Section (150-200 words)**
   - 4-6 key benefits
   - Focus on outcomes (curb appeal, property value, safety)

3. **Process Section (100-150 words)**
   - How it works (simple 3-4 steps)
   - Professional equipment mention
   - Safety/care emphasis

4. **Local Relevance (100-150 words)**
   - Why this service matters in West Sussex
   - Common issues in the area (moss, algae, weather-related)
   - Types of properties served

5. **Pricing Transparency (50-100 words)**
   - Price range or factors
   - Link to pricing page
   - "Free quote" CTA

6. **Social Proof (50-100 words)**
   - Testimonial placeholder
   - Trust signals (insured, experienced)

7. **CTA Section (50 words)**
   - Strong call to action
   - Multiple contact options
   - Urgency/scarcity if applicable
