Common Patio Problems in Surrey

Black Spots on Patios

Black spots are one of the most frustrating patio problems for homeowners in Redhill, Reigate and surrounding areas. These dark stains are typically caused by Lichen planus, a fungus that embeds itself in porous patio materials. Our specialist treatment targets these black spots effectively, removing them and preventing rapid regrowth.

Slippery Algae Growth

Surrey's damp climate creates perfect conditions for green algae to flourish on patios, especially in shaded areas. This creates dangerous slippery surfaces that are hazardous to walk on. Our pressure washing removes all algae growth, making your patio safe for family use.

Moss Between Paving

Moss loves to grow in the joints between patio slabs, creating an unkempt appearance and potentially lifting slabs if left unchecked. We thoroughly remove moss and can provide re-pointing services if required.

General Dirt & Staining

Over time, patios accumulate dirt, leaf stains, bird droppings, and general weathering that make them look dull and neglected. Professional cleaning restores the original appearance dramatically.

Our Patio Cleaning Process

1. Surface Assessment

2. Weed & Moss Removal

We remove weeds and moss from joints between slabs, ensuring thorough cleaning right down to the foundation.

3. Pre-Treatment Application

Stubborn black spots and heavy organic growth receive specialist biocide treatment to break down staining effectively.

4. Pressure Washing

5. Finishing & Protection
